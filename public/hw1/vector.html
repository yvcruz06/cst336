Vector Format

Main Raster Vector Compound


Vector images are constructed using mathematical formulas rather than individual colored blocks

EPS, AI, SVG and PDF* are some formats that are good for creating graphics that require resizing.

They are also good to use for Logos and brand graphics

Smaller items like business card and letterhead use vector images

Larger surfaces, such as your corporate jet and banners are best seen with vector formats



Examples:
  • EPS
  • AI
  • SVG
  • PDF
  • CGM
  • RS-274X
