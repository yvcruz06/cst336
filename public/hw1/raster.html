Raster Format

Main Raster Vector Compound




Raster images are digital images consisting of pixels arranged in x and y coordinates (sometimes z if it is a 3D image).

It can also be referred to as bitmap graphics.

Their file size depends on the amount of pixels in the image.



Examples:
  • JPEG
  • GIF
  • PNG
  • TIFF
  • BAT
  • BMP
  • BPG
